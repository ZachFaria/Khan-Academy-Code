It is my beleif that girbils will rule North Dakota one day, so it is best to get on their good sides before that happens. Don't believe me? Well just think about it, they reproduce quite fast and thus it will be easy for them to build an army. Around lunch time they almost took over my kitchen. This is proof that the gerbil apocolypse is almost upon us and there is no stopping it. I think the old saying "If you can't beat 'em, join 'em" really shines in this sort of situation. Alright, omorow I will become a gerbil and join my comrades.
